Sataplia Cave

Sataplia Cave is located in the Imereti region of Georgia and is famous for its dinosaur footprints and beautiful stalactites and stalagmites. The cave is part of a larger nature reserve which includes a museum and a glass bridge with stunning views.

Prometheus Cave

Prometheus Cave, also located in Imereti, is one of Georgia's natural wonders. The cave features breathtaking formations, underground rivers, and lakes. Guided tours provide a mesmerizing experience with beautiful lighting enhancing the natural beauty of the cave.

Martvili Canyon

Martvili Canyon, situated in the Samegrelo region, offers visitors a chance to explore its stunning waterfalls and crystal-clear waters. Boat tours are available, allowing you to enjoy the canyon's serene beauty up close. It's a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.
